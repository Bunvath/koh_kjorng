Koh Kjong Farm

Amongst all agricultural lands in Sihanouk province, Kohkjong farm stands out to offer you a taste of rural livings with wonderful scenery unsurpassed in today's hustle and bustle of city life.

At here, we are committed to deliver excellent service and quality products. We are working from heart to make your experience worthwhile and memorable. We envision ourselves as a widely known farm resort to be your first choice when it comes to agri-tourist destination.
